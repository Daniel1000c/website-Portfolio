It's All About Them

Fostering Families and Communities

Broward Rescue is a non-profit organization dedicated to rescuing and rehoming animals in need. We provide a safe haven for abandoned, abused, and homeless pets, ensuring they receive the care and love they deserve. Our mission is to match these animals with caring families, creating lasting bonds and giving them a second chance at a happy life.
