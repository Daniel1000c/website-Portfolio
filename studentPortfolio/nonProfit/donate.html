Every Donation Helps

Sometimes it is very hard to believe that a couple of dollars can really make a difference. At Broward Rescue, every single cent means that an mistreated animal can all the much needed TLC they can endure. Broward Rescue is a financially independent nonprofit entity. We soley rely on the generosity of the members in the community. We are not affiliated with any government programs.

Single Gifts

You may choose any dollar gift amount today. A single donation has the power to provide essential living needs to our animals such as food, shelter, and medical care to animals in need. At Broward Rescue, we are dedicated to supporting individuals and Broward Rescue families who are facing hardships, homelessness, or even hunger. Your one-time donation will directly impact animal lives and pave a path for a brighter tomorrow for our pets.

Monthly Sustainer Gifts

Show your pet love all year round. Becoming a Broward Rescue monthly supporter is the best way to start off payments small and build up your monthly gifts or breaking bigger payments into smaller payments. Rest assured, your credit card will be securely charged each billing month, and you can change or opt out of this option at any time.
