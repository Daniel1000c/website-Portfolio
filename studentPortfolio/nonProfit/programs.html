Welcome to our Rescue Programs!

Haven for Pets

At Broward Rescue, our adoption, youth education programs, and animal health services aim to foster a deep and founding connection between pets and their families. We're thrilled to share Broward Rescue's mission and happily introduce you to the wonderful animals in facility care. The nature of these programs is to help nurture, inspire and have the community engage with the active process of taking care of the welfare of our precious animals.

Our Programs

🐾Shelter Tour

Broward Rescue offers at facility shelter tours for various school groups and other neighborhood community groups. Tour groups are engaged in an active process of rescuing and rehoming pets.

🐾ClinicTour

Broward Rescue offers at facility clinic tours for groups that will be engaged with the process of rescuing and rehoming pets.

🐾Youth Programs

When school is out, Come to down to Broward rescue and give your love and attention to our lovely pet friends.

🐾Pet Camp Day

Teens and adults will learn about various positive dog training techniques, working task in the Broward Rescue Shelter and a very small amount of animal clinic care. The participants will spend a good amount of time interacting with large dogs, cats, and various other small animals.
